First Round Tryouts


You have the option to try out for the team as an attorney, witness, or both. Though you may feel that you are particularly suited for one or the other, we always encourage trying out as both. A form for selecting tryout slots will be presented at the Open House and Demo, and the link will be uploaded to this site afterwards and emailed to the interest listserv. The first round auditions should last approximately five minutes per person. The sign-up sheet will be organized into 30-minute blocks of time from Tuesday to Wednesday. Six people may sign up for each block, and then participants will be called in one at a time, first-come first serve, within those thirty minutes.

The first-round audition for attorneys will consist of one part:

  • Delivering an argumentative speech based on the case materials provided.

The first-round audition for witnesses will consist of two parts:

  • Answering an array of questions asked by one of our attorneys, in character, as a witness (based on provided materials). Explain your side of the story in your responses - and don't let the attorney trip you up!
