Tryouts


We host tryouts for each season early in the fall semester. This year's tryouts will be held September 20th (Tuesday) and September 21st (Wednesday). Information about the tryouts is available at our open houses and on our tryouts page. We will also be emailing tryout logistics, so make sure to sign up for information!

We encourage you to reach out to our current members to speak about their mock trial experiences.

For other tryout-related questions, please email our Recruitment Chair, Regina Zeng.
